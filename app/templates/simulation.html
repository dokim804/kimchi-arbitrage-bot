Profitability Simulation

This simulation takes into account the current up-to-date Bitcoin prices in two different platforms (European vs Korean), and returns profit & ROI with given arbitrage amount. Put the amount of money you'd like to use for the arbitrage transaction.

The scenario is that you send KRW from a Korean account to buy Bitcoins on a European Bitcoin platform. For this, you need to transfer money from Korean to European account. Afterwards, you will transfer Bitcoin from the European to the Korean Bitcoin platform, sell it, and make profit.
