Korean Premium Arbitrage Bot

Track the Kimchi Premium—the price difference of Bitcoin between Korean and global exchanges—and simulate real-world arbitrage opportunities.

The Korean crypto market often shows a higher BTC price than Western markets. This dashboard lets you:

  • Visualize real-time Bitcoin prices from both Korean and European exchanges, and monitor the Kimchi Premium as it changes over time.
  • Simulate an arbitrage trade step-by-step, including all realistic fees, slippage, and transfer times, to estimate your potential profit and ROI.
  • Understand the impact of market friction—such as conversion fees, trading fees, network fees, and delays—on your arbitrage strategy.

Use the tabs above to explore the Bitcoin price tracker or try the Profitability Simulation tool.

This project demonstrates real-time data integration, financial modeling, and full-stack development for cross-border crypto trading.
