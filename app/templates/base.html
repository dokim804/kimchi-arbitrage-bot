Korean Premium Arbitrage Dashboard

This dashboard helps you track the "Kimchi Premium"—the price difference of Bitcoin between Korean and global exchanges—and simulate potential arbitrage profits.

  • Bitcoin price tracker: Visualizes real-time BTC prices and the Korean premium over time.
  • Profitability Simulation: Lets you simulate an arbitrage trade, including all fees, slippage, and transfer times, to estimate profit and ROI.

Use the tabs above to switch between the price tracker and the simulation tool.
